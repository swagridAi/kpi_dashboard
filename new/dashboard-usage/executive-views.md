# Executive Dashboard Views

## Strategic Overview Dashboard

### Key Metrics Display

The executive dashboard provides organizational service performance through three primary views:

**Organizational SLO Performance**
- Current month achievement rate across all capabilities
- Six-month rolling average (smooths monthly variations)
- Performance against 95% organizational target
- Visual indicators: Green (95%+), Yellow (85-94%), Red (<85%)

**Cross-Capability Comparisons**
- Side-by-side performance for Data Quality, Data Extracts, Change Controls, Reference Data, and Records Management
- Capability maturity scoring based on consistency and improvement trends
- Resource utilization and ticket volume analysis
- Best-performing teams identified for knowledge sharing

**Trend Indicators and Forecasts**
- Month-over-month change with directional arrows
- Seasonal patterns for capacity planning
- Early warning indicators for at-risk capabilities
- Predictive insights based on current trajectory

### Reading the Executive Scorecard

**Performance Indicators:**
- **SLO Achievement Rate**: Primary metric showing percentage of tickets resolved within target timeframes
- **Month-over-Month Change**: Improvement (+) or decline (-) from previous month
- **Six-Month Average**: Long-term trend indicator, smooths seasonal variations
- **Tickets at Risk**: Open tickets approaching SLO deadlines

**Interpretation Guide:**
- Green: Exceeding expectations, sustainable performance
- Yellow: Meeting minimums, improvement opportunities exist
- Red: Immediate attention required, potential customer impact

## Monthly Executive Reports

### Automated Report Contents

Delivered first business day of each month:
- **Executive Summary**: Overall SLO achievement vs. previous month and targets
- **Capability Performance**: Best/worst performers with explanatory context
- **Trend Analysis**: Six-month patterns with seasonal adjustments
- **Action Items**: Data-driven recommendations for underperforming areas
- **Resource Insights**: Capacity utilization and optimization opportunities

### Key Questions to Ask

**Performance Assessment:**
- Which capabilities are consistently above/below target?
- What seasonal patterns impact our delivery capacity?
- Are we meeting our customer service commitments?

**Resource Planning:**
- Where will targeted investment yield highest returns?
- Which teams need additional support or training?
- Can we reallocate capacity between capabilities?

**Strategic Alignment:**
- How does SLO performance support business objectives?
- What capabilities are critical for upcoming initiatives?

## Using SLO Data for Decision Making

### Resource Allocation
- **Bottleneck Analysis**: Use ticket flow data to identify capacity constraints
- **Investment Prioritization**: Focus automation on high-volume, low-SLO capabilities
- **Team Optimization**: Correlate team size with throughput and SLO achievement

### Strategic Planning
- **Capacity Forecasting**: Use trends to predict future resource needs
- **Service Expansion**: Assess capability readiness for additional services
- **Customer Commitments**: Align external SLAs with demonstrated internal performance

### Capability Investment
- **ROI Analysis**: Compare improvement potential against investment required
- **Risk Mitigation**: Prioritize capabilities critical to business operations
- **Innovation Pilots**: Leverage high-performing capabilities for process innovation

## Executive Alert Management

**Critical Alerts** (immediate notification):
- Multiple capabilities below 85% achievement
- Month-over-month decline >10%
- Customer-facing services at SLA breach risk

**Strategic Alerts** (daily digest):
- Capacity approaching operational limits
- Cross-capability improvement opportunities
- Exceptional performance recognition opportunities

**Configuration Options:**
- SMS/Teams for critical issues
- Email digest for strategic alerts
- Weekly briefings for trend analysis

## Sharing with Stakeholders

### Best Practices for Communication
- **Provide Context**: Always explain business impact of metrics
- **Focus on Trends**: Emphasize improvement direction over absolute numbers
- **Include Actions**: Specify next steps with performance data
- **Celebrate Wins**: Highlight achievements and improvement stories
- **Address Challenges**: Present issues honestly with mitigation plans

### Stakeholder-Specific Views
- **Board Reports**: Quarterly summaries with strategic recommendations
- **Customer Updates**: Monthly transparency reports with improvement communications
- **Internal Teams**: Performance data contextualized for operational relevance

The executive dashboard transforms operational SLO data into strategic insights, enabling informed decisions that optimize service delivery and resource allocation across the organization.
