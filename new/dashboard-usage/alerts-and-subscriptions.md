# Alerts and Subscriptions Management

Stay informed about your team's SLO performance with intelligent notifications designed to provide actionable insights without overwhelming you.

## Types of Alerts

### SLO Breach Alerts
**Triggered when:** Performance falls below defined SLO targets  
**Immediate actions:** Review affected tickets, identify root causes, implement corrective measures, and communicate response plans to stakeholders.

### Risk Alerts  
**Triggered when:** Performance approaches SLO thresholds (typically 80% of target)  
**Prevention focus:** Proactively address at-risk tickets, redistribute workload, escalate complex issues, and review process bottlenecks.

### Trend Alerts
**Triggered when:** Significant performance pattern changes occur  
**Examples:** Declining SLO rates, unusual volume spikes, resolution time changes, or quality metric deterioration.

### Capacity Alerts
**Triggered when:** Volume spikes require immediate attention  
**Response:** Assess resource availability, prioritize critical tickets, consider temporary reallocation, and plan capacity expansion.

## Setting Up Alerts

### One-Click Setup
Use role-based templates for immediate value:
- **Executive**: Strategic performance alerts and organizational SLO breaches
- **Capability Owner**: Team performance, risk alerts, and trend notifications  
- **Team Member**: Personal workload alerts and contribution metrics

### Personal Configuration
Access your alert preferences through the Confluence self-service interface:

1. **Navigate** to your team's SLO configuration page
2. **Select alert types** relevant to your responsibilities
3. **Set thresholds** that trigger notifications
4. **Choose delivery methods** (email, mobile, Teams, SMS)
5. **Test setup** using the preview feature

### Team-Level Configuration
Capability owners can establish team-wide notifications:
- Configure Microsoft Teams channel alerts
- Set up escalation rules for senior management
- Define business hours for alert timing
- Customize notifications by team member role

## Anti-Fatigue Features

### Intelligent Suppression
- Prevents duplicate alerts for the same issue
- Filters minor fluctuations that don't require action
- Learns from your response patterns to improve relevance

### Smart Batching
- Groups related alerts into consolidated summaries
- Delivers notifications at optimal times
- Provides cross-issue context for better decision-making

### Dynamic Thresholds
- Automatically adjusts sensitivity based on historical performance
- Adapts to seasonal patterns and workload variations
- Incorporates user feedback to optimize alert value

## Best Practices

### Initial Setup
- **Start with role-based templates** for immediate relevance
- **Begin conservatively** with higher thresholds, then adjust based on experience
- **Enable smart features** to reduce notification noise
- **Test thoroughly** before activating all alert types

### Ongoing Optimization
- **Review monthly** to assess alert effectiveness
- **Track response rates** to identify valuable vs. noise alerts
- **Gather team feedback** about alert frequency and content
- **Adjust seasonally** for known busy or slow periods

### Team Coordination
- **Align individual preferences** with team channel notifications
- **Configure backup alerts** for when team members are unavailable
- **Share best practices** across team members
- **Establish escalation protocols** for critical issues

## Troubleshooting Common Issues

### Not Receiving Alerts
- Verify subscription settings in your Confluence profile
- Check email spam/junk folders and mobile notification settings
- Confirm Teams channel permissions and integration status
- Validate system connectivity and processing status

### Alert Overload
- Increase thresholds to reduce sensitivity
- Enable smart batching for consolidated updates
- Review and disable unnecessary alert types
- Move less urgent updates to weekly digest format

### Poor Alert Timing
- Verify time zone settings in your profile
- Check business hours configuration
- Review system performance for processing delays
- Consider adjusting dynamic threshold sensitivity

### Missing Context
- Enable detailed descriptions in alert preferences
- Configure dashboard links in notification templates
- Add business justification to threshold settings
- Review alert rules for appropriate context inclusion
